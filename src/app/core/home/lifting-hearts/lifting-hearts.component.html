SUPPORT THE CAUSE BY “LIFTING HEARTS”

With a common goal to be healthy survivors and to help other survivors heal, the group
Lifting Hearts was organized to provide mentoring, monthly support meetings, breast cancer
retreats, service opportunities, and family activities, all aimed at sharing support, wisdom,
laughter and hope.  Each woman who joins Lifting Hearts gains instant friendships with
women who have walked in her path.  She finds a place to celebrate today and
figure out tomorrow, whatever comes. Become a part of the cause.
